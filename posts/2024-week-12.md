  U.S. hotel performance increased from the previous week but declined year over year, according to CoStarâ€™s latest data through 9 March.

  - The steepest RevPAR declines were seen in Las Vegas (-30.6% to US$123.99) and Anaheim (-26.2% to US$139.21).
