## [U.S. hotel results for week ending 30 May](https://www.costar.com/products/str-benchmark/resources/press-releases/us-hotel-results-week-ending-6-june)

The U.S. hotel industry reported positive year-over-year comparisons, according to CoStar’s latest data through 6 June.

- Among the Top 25 Markets, Philadelphia saw the highest occupancy increase (+9.8% to 75.3%).
- Chicago posted the largest gains in ADR (+14.5% to US$237.53) and RevPAR (+23.9% to US$198.21), helped by the U.S. Men’s National Team World Cup send-off match.
- Overall, 21 of the Top 25 Markets saw year-over-year RevPAR growth.
- Despite hosting CMA Fest, Nashville reported the steepest RevPAR decline (-7.5% to US$138.99).

31 May - 6 June 2026 (percentage change from comparable week in 2025):

- Occupancy: 67.9% (+1.3%)
- Average daily rate (ADR): US$168.43 (+4.0%)
- Revenue per available room (RevPAR): US$114.30 (+5.3%)
