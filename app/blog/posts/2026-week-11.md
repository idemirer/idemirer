## [U.S. hotel results for week ending 7 March](https://www.costar.com/products/benchmark/resources/press-releases/us-hotel-results-week-ending-14-march)

The U.S. hotel industry reported positive year-over-year comparisons, according to CoStar’s latest data through 14 March.

- Among the Top 25 Markets, San Francisco reported the highest increases across each of the three key performance metrics: occupancy (+22.1% to 77.5%), ADR (+34.6% to US$263.43) and RevPAR (+64.4% to US$204.08). The market’s performance was lifted by the Game Developers Conference.
- World Baseball Classic host, Miami, saw the second-largest performance gains: occupancy (+10.1% to 87.3%), ADR (+17.0% to US$315.14) and RevPAR (+28.9% to US$275.04).
- Overall, 20 of the Top 25 Markets saw a lift in RevPAR.

8-14 March 2026 (percentage change from comparable week in 2025):

- Occupancy: 65.7% (+2.3%)
- Average daily rate (ADR): US$167.97 (+3.2%)
- Revenue per available room (RevPAR): US$110.29 (+5.6%)
