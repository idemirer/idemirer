## [U.S. hotel results for week ending 17 January](https://www.costar.com/products/benchmark/resources/press-releases/us-hotel-results-week-ending-17-january)

The U.S. hotel industry reported positive year-over-year comparisons, according to CoStar’s latest data through 17 January.

- Among the Top 25 Markets, Minneapolis reported the largest occupancy increase (+12.2% to 51.6%) and the second-highest jump in RevPAR (+17.4% to US$63.93).
- Miami saw the highest ADR gain (+11.8% to US$287.54), due to the impact of the College Football Playoff National Championship.
- Philadelphia registered the largest lift in RevPAR (+21.8% to US$86.68).
- Overall, 16 of the Top 25 Markets saw an increase in RevPAR.
- Washington, D.C. reported the most pronounced decreases in ADR (-31.3%) and RevPAR (-32.1% to US$93.43) with difficult year-over-year comparisons from the weekend of last year’s Presidential Inauguration.

11-17 January 2026 (percentage change from comparable week in 2025):

- Occupancy: 56.1% (+0.7%)
- Average daily rate (ADR): US$157.21 (+0.9%)
- Revenue per available room (RevPAR): US$88.19 (+1.6%)
