## [U.S. hotel results for week ending 21 March](https://www.hotelmanagement.net/data-trends/us-weekly-hotel-results-march-15-21)

The U.S. hotel industry reported positive year-over-year comparisons, according to CoStar’s latest data.

- Among CoStar’s top 25 markets, Denver reported the largest increases in occupancy (+19.1 percent to 73.2 percent) and RevPAR (+30.7 percent to $104.94). The market’s performance was helped by the APS Global Physics Summit.
- March Madness host, St. Louis, posted the highest ADR gain (+15.5 percent to $137.38) and the second-largest RevPAR lift (+29.6 percent to $99.13).
- Overall, 18 of CoStar’s top 25 markets saw a lift in RevPAR.

15-21 March 2026 (percentage change from comparable week in 2025):

- Occupancy: 67.7% (+2.7%)
- Average daily rate (ADR): US$169.02 (+2.2%)
- Revenue per available room (RevPAR): US$114.44 (+4.9%)
