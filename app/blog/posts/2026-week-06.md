## [U.S. hotel results for week ending 7 February](https://www.costar.com/products/benchmark/resources/press-releases/us-hotel-results-week-ending-7-february)

The U.S. hotel industry reported positive year-over-year comparisons, according to CoStar’s latest data through 7 February.

- Among the Top 25 Markets, San Francisco reported the largest increases across each of the three key performance metrics: occupancy (+33.5% to 78.7%), ADR (+108.3% to US$409.25) and RevPAR (+178.1% to US$322.07). The market’s performance was due to the impact of Super Bowl LX.
- Last year’s Super Bowl host, New Orleans, registered the most pronounced performance decreases due to a comparison against the 2025 game weekend: occupancy (-22.4% to 57.5%), ADR (-65.4% to US$170.46), and RevPAR (-73.1% to US$98.08).

1-7 February 2026 (percentage change from comparable week in 2025):

- Occupancy: 56.4% (+1.1%)
- Average daily rate (ADR): US$158.69 (+1.7%)
- Revenue per available room (RevPAR): US$89.55 (+2.8%)
