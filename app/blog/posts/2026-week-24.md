## [U.S. hotel results for week ending 13 June](https://www.costar.com/products/str-benchmark/resources/press-releases/us-hotel-results-week-ending-13-june)

The U.S. hotel industry reported positive year-over-year comparisons for the week ending June 13, according to data from CoStar.

- Among the top 25 markets, Detroit saw the highest occupancy increase (+7.9% to 71%).
- New York City posted the largest gains in ADR (+17.1% to $399.15) and RevPAR (+18.9% to $358.00), helped by the New York Knicks in the NBA Finals and a World Cup match.
- Los Angeles, host of the USA vs. Paraguay World Cup match, reported the second-highest ADR lift (+13.7% to $234.02).

7-13 June 2026 (percentage change from comparable week in 2025):

- Occupancy: 69.9% (+1.9%)
- Average daily rate (ADR): US$172.04 (+4.9%)
- Revenue per available room (RevPAR): US$120.34 (+7.0%)
