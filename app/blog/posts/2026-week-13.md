## [U.S. hotel results for week ending 28 March](https://www.costar.com/products/str-benchmark/resources/press-releases/us-hotel-results-week-ending-28-march)

The U.S. hotel industry reported positive year-over-year comparisons, according to CoStar’s latest data through 28 March.

- Among the Top 25 Markets, San Francisco reported the largest increases in each of the three key performance metrics: occupancy (+26.5% to 83.6%), ADR (+74.8% to US$339.47) and RevPAR (+121.1% to US$283.76). The market’s performance was helped by the 35th annual RSA Conference.
- Denver, which hosted the American Academy of Dermatology Annual Meeting, posted the second largest performance gains: occupancy (+20.2% to 71.1%), ADR (+23.5% to US$158.39) and RevPAR (+48.5% to US$112.56).
- Overall, 18 of the Top 25 Markets saw a lift in RevPAR.

22-28 March 2026 (percentage change from comparable week in 2025):

- Occupancy: 66.8% (+2.8%)
- Average daily rate (ADR): US$170.30 (+5.4%)
- Revenue per available room (RevPAR): US$113.81 (+8.3%)
